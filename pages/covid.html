The salon has new guidelines that we will be following that are mandated by NYS. It is very important that you feel comfortable and safe at our salon. Please do not hesitate to reschedule for a later date if you are uncomfortable coming in, we understand and will do our best to find you a new appointment date. Please reschedule if you are not feeling well.

COVID-19 GUIDELINES

  • A facemask will be required during your entire service.
  • Your temperature may be taken with a touchless thermometer and kept on file.
  • You must wash and sanitize your hands upon arrival.
  • A waiver will need to be signed when you arrive. You can download that by clicking  here.
  • Please text or call us when you arrive, and we will let you know when you can come in. You can wait in your car or on our outdoor porch.
  • We will be cleaning and sanitizing between each appointment, which is why your wait time may be longer than you are used to.
  • For joint appointments, please come in one at a time, unless you are bringing in a child under the age of 12 for a hair service.
  • We will be unable to take walk-in appointments at this time.
  • The shampoo area will be a quiet zone, we will be keeping conversations to a minimum. This is temporary unless you really like it.
  • If you would like coffee, tea, or water, please let us know and we will be more than happy to get it for you.
  • Please bring in your own reading material and take it with you.

These guidelines are temporary, and we apologize for being so restrictive, but we are so excited to be able to see you and to be working in the salon again.

Please do not hesitate to reach out to any of us with any questions that you may have.
